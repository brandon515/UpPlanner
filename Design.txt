request people to join you in an event

data required:
	Name of event
	participants
	place/game
	time
	people required(optional)
	max amount of people(optional)

valid data:
	Name of event:
		any string
	participants:
		data from contact lists or a raw number, this is what smsmanager will use
	place/game:
		any string
	time:
		the hour(1-12) and the minute(0-60) or when a certain number of people have accepted
	people required:
		the lower limit of people, is only an option when the second choice for time has been chosen
	max amount of people:
		upper limit of people, when this number has been accepted the list will notify and block any chance to accept an event
